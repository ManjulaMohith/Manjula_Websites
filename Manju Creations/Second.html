The Best Special FX Makeup Courses

Cinema Makeup School

Cinema Makeup offers an on-ground four-week SFX makeup class that teaches you everything you want to know about professional SFX makeup. No experience is necessary to take the course, but they do recommend that students have some experiences in sculpting or molding. You can either take those courses first or submit your portfolio to prove you have that experience.Topics included in the course range from sculpting and dental appliances to blood and finishing.

Online Makeup Academy

This online course enables you to learn SFX makeup at home, without traveling to schools in New York or Los Angeles. Instructors are based in New York and provide students with one-on-one guidance and mentorship. The goal of the course is to help you build a professional quality portfolio and to help you land a professional gig

The course includes a makeup kit enabling you to apply what you learn. Throughout the course, you will complete projects and submit them to the instructor for review. Being a student enables you to take advantage of a Pro Card discount program on makeup and supplies.
